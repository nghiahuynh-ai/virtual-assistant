Innotech was established in 2014 and is 10 years old now. 
Innotech is primarily operating in industries such as Warehouse, Fintech, Human Resources, AI Tech, and Insurtech. 
Innotech mainly offers services such as Mobile app, Web app, Frontend, and Backend. 
Innotech's main office is located at 33 Ba Vi Street, Ward 4, Tan Binh District, Ho Chi Minh City. 
Innotech currently has 5 offices which are located in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Binh Duong, and Texas. 
At Innotech, the working day start from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. 
"Innotech" term is the combination of "Innovative" and "Technology"; "Inno" symbolizes creativity and innovation, while "Tech" represents technological solutions and advancements. 
The reason why Innotech's logo is in orange and black is that orange is associated with dynamism, energy, and creativity, representing the commitment to creating breakthrough solutions; while black conveys professionalism and reliability.
The core values of Innotech inludes Humility, Co-Ownership, Integrity, Go-Getting and Go-Giving, Dedication. 
Innotech has undertaken over 200 software transformations and assisted hundreds of businesses in offering a more contemporary, user-friendly digital experience to their customers.
